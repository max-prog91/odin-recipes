Delicious Homemade Apple Pie: A Timeless Classic

Indulge in the comforting flavors of homemade apple pie, a classic dessert that brings warmth and nostalgia to any gathering. This recipe features a flaky, buttery crust filled with tender, spiced apples that are perfectly sweetened and baked to golden perfection. Each slice offers a delightful balance of tart and sweet, enhanced by a hint of cinnamon and nutmeg. Whether served warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or at room temperature, this apple pie is sure to be a crowd-pleaser. Perfect for holidays, family gatherings, or simply a cozy night in, this recipe will become a cherished favorite in your home.

Ingredients

Fore the Pie Crust

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsalted butter (cold, cut into cubes)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 6-8 tbsp ice water

For the Apple Filling

  • 6-8 medium apples (a mix of sweet and tart, such as Granny Smith and Honeycrisp)
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp butter (to dot on top)

How to make a pie

  1. Prepare the Pie Crust

    In a large bowl, combine flour, salt, and sugar. Add cold butter and mix until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Gradually add ice water, one tablespoon at a time, mixing until the dough comes together. Divide the dough in half, shape into discs, wrap in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

  2. Make the Apple Filling:

    Peel, core, and slice the apples. In a large bowl, combine sliced apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice. Toss to coat evenly.

  3. Assemble the Pie:

    Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Roll out one disc of dough on a floured surface to fit your pie pan. Place it in the pan. Fill the crust with the apple mixture and dot with butter. Roll out the second disc of dough and place it over the filling. Seal and flute the edges. Cut slits in the top crust for steam to escape.

  4. Bake the Pie:

    Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes. Reduce the temperature to 350°F (175°C) and continue baking for 35-45 minutes, or until the apples are tender and the crust is golden brown.

  5. Cool and Serve:

    Allow the pie to cool for at least 2 hours before slicing. Serve warm or at room temperature, optionally with vanilla ice cream.
