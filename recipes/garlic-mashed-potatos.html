Germany Garlic Mashed Potatos

Experience the ultimate comfort food with these creamy garlic mashed potatoes. Perfectly fluffy and infused with the rich flavor of roasted garlic, this dish is a delightful side that pairs well with any meal. The combination of buttery potatoes and aromatic garlic creates a savory, indulgent treat that will elevate your dining experience. Whether for a holiday feast or a weeknight dinner, these garlic mashed potatoes are sure to be a hit!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds russet or Yukon Gold potatoes
  • 4 cloves garlic (peeled)
  • ½ cup unsalted butter (at room temperature)
  • ½ cup heavy cream (or milk)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (to taste)
  • Fresh chives or parsley (for garnish, optional)

How to make steps.

  1. Prepare the Potatoes:

    Peel the potatoes and cut them into evenly sized chunks (about 1-2 inches). Place the potato chunks and garlic cloves in a large pot and cover with cold water. Add a pinch of salt.

  2. Cook the Potatoes:

    Bring the pot to a boil over medium-high heat. Once boiling, reduce the heat to medium and simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the potatoes are fork-tender.

  3. Drain and Steam:

    Drain the potatoes and garlic in a colander. Allow them to sit for a few minutes to let the steam escape, which helps to keep the potatoes fluffy.

  4. Mash the Potatoes:

    Return the drained potatoes and garlic to the pot. Use a potato masher or a ricer to mash the potatoes until smooth and creamy.

  5. Add Butter and Cream:

    Stir in the room temperature butter and heavy cream (or milk) until fully incorporated. Adjust the consistency by adding more cream if desired.

  6. Season:

    Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Mix well to combine.

  7. Serve:

    Transfer the garlic mashed potatoes to a serving bowl. Garnish with chopped fresh chives or parsley if desired. Serve warm.
