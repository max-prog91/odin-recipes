Classic Omelette

A classic omelette is a simple yet versatile dish that makes for a perfect breakfast, brunch, or light dinner. This fluffy and tender omelette is made with just a few basic ingredients, allowing the rich flavor of the eggs to shine through. You can customize it with your favorite fillings, such as cheese, herbs, vegetables, or meats, making it a delightful option for any palate. Quick to prepare and satisfying, this omelette is a staple in kitchens around the world.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk or water
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Optional fillings (e.g., shredded cheese, diced vegetables, cooked ham, fresh herbs)

Preparing steps

  1. Prepare the Eggs:

    In a medium bowl, crack the eggs and add the milk (or water). Season with salt and black pepper. Whisk until the mixture is well combined and slightly frothy.

  2. Heat the Pan:

    Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add the butter and let it melt, swirling it around to coat the bottom of the pan.

  3. Cock the Eggs:

    Pour the egg mixture into the skillet. Let it cook undisturbed for about 1-2 minutes, or until the edges start to set.

  4. Stir and Fold:

    Using a spatula, gently stir the eggs, pushing the cooked edges toward the center while tilting the pan to allow the uncooked eggs to flow to the edges. Continue this process until the eggs are mostly set but still slightly runny on top.

  5. Add Fillings:

    If using fillings, sprinkle them evenly over one half of the omelette.
